DOM Traversal

Lightsaber

A lightsaber is a plasma blade emitted from a hilt through a kyber crystal, which can be shut off at will. It can cut through almost anything, including blast doors, but takes specialized training to use well.

Hoverboard

A hoverboard is a skateboard that hovers above the ground and moves forward under its own power.

Replicator

A replicator is a device that transforms any matter, including waste or trash, into any other object or material. It can break anything down into its component atoms and rearrange those atoms into any shape.
